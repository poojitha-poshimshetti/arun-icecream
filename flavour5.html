CHOCOLATE ROLLED ICECREAM

Rolled ice cream is not pulled from frozen buckets by scoop like regular ice cream. Instead, its liquid base is poured pan and then chopped, mixed,and rolled. Although it takes more time.



STRAWBERRY ROLLED ICECREAM

Rolled ice-cream is a process in-which a base flavor of cream is chosen and combined with any chosen ingredients to be mixed, flattened, and rolled into the popular dessert we love to serve.



VANILLA ROLLED ICECREAM

Vanilla rolled ice cream is a frozen dessert made with a vanilla ice cream base that's mixed with fresh ingredients, frozen cold surface, and then rolled up into the popular dessert we love to serve.



BUTTERSCOTCH ROLLED ICECREAM

Butterscotch rolled ice cream is made by pouring liquid butterscotch ice cream onto a frozen surface, then chopping, mixing, spreading, and rolling it. Butterscotch ice cream has a and sweet flavor.



OREO ROLLED ICECREAM

Oreo Ice Cream Roll is a frozen dessert made with vanilla ice cream, Oreo pieces, This OREO ice cream roll is delicious combination of vanilla-flavored ice cream blended cookies then rolled.
