FLAVOURS


CHOCOLATE STICK ICECREAM

Chocolate ice cream is generally made by blending cocoa powder, and the eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar used to make vanilla ice cream. Sometimes chocolate liquor is used addition to cocoa powder.



STRAWBERRY STICK ICECREAM

Strawberry ice cream is a flavor of ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, and sugar.



VANILLA STICK ICECREAM

Vanilla ice cream is a classic flavor commonly made with vanilla extract, cream, milk, and sugar. It has a simple, sweet taste and creamy texture. Vanilla ice cream is perfect on its own and creamy.



BUTTERSCOTCH STICK ICECREAM

Butterscotch ice cream typically has a rich, smooth flavor with notes of browned butter, dark brown sugar, vanilla and in many cases bourbon. It has a smooth, and caramelized taste with a buttery.



OREO STICK ICECREAM

Oreo ice cream sticks are a frozen dessert made with vanilla ice cream, Oreo cookie pieces, and a chocolate coating.coated in crushed chocolate flavour cookies with a vanilla flavour filling .
