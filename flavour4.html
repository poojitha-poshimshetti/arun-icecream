FLAVOURS


CHOCOLATE KULFI

Chocolate ice cream is generally made by blending cocoa powder, and the eggs, cream, vanilla, and sugar used to make vanilla ice cream. Sometimes chocolate liquor in addition to cocoa powder.



STRAWBERRY KULFI

Strawberry ice cream is a flavor of ice cream made with strawberry or strawberry flavoring. It is made by blending in fresh strawberries or strawberry flavoring with the eggs, cream, vanilla.



VANILLA KULFI

Kulfi is a cold dessert made from dairy, which is just frozen. The mixture is usually flavoured milk cardamom, saffron, pistachio, rose that slowly cooked in a kadai which reduces the milk.



BUTTERSCOTCH KULFI

The base is made with cream (and milk sometimes) and it has crunchy the praline swirled into it. The crunchy praline give the ice cream an amazing texture.the nuts in butterscotch ice cream.



OREO KULFI

Oreo ice cream, also known as cookies and cream ice cream, is a favorite of just about everyone. My recipe is made with a traditional custard base but isn't your average vanilla ice cream.
